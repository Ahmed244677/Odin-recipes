Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese—hearty, cheesy, and delicious.

INGREDIENTS

  • Ground beef (500g)
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato sauce (400g)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese (optional)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook meat with onion, garlic, and tomato sauce.
  2. Layer sauce, noodles, cheese (repeat).
  3. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 40 mins.
  4. Rest 10 mins, then serve.
