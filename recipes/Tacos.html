Tacos

Description:

French tacos are grilled tortilla wraps stuffed with meat, fries, and cheesy sauce—crispy outside, melty inside, and super filling!

INGREDIENTS

  • Large tortilla wrap
  • Cooked meat (chicken, beef, etc.)
  • French fries
  • Cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, etc.)
  • Cheese sauce or crème fraîche
  • Optional: spices, harissa, veggies

STEPS

  • Layer meat, fries, cheese & sauce on tortilla.
  • Fold tightly like a wrap.
  • Grill in a pan or press until crispy.
  • Cut & enjoy.
